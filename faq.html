Frequently asked questions

General

How does SwapReads work?
SwapReads is a global book exchange platform where users can create profiles, list the books they're willing to swap, and find other users' collections. When you find a book you're interested in, you can find suitable offers and read that book. It's that easy!
How does SwapReads contribute to sustainability?
By promoting book reuse, SwapReads reduces waste and encourages a circular economy where books find new owners instead of being discarded.
How do I join the SwapReads community?
You can join SwapReads by signing up on our website, creating a profile, and listing books you are willing to exchange.
How do I ensure the safety and security of my personal information on SwapReads?
SwapReads uses encryption and privacy protection measures to keep your personal data secure. You should also follow best practices like using strong passwords.
